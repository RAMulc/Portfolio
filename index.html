Robert Mulcrone

Robert is an engineer with over twenty years’ experience in the design and documentation of overhead wiring systems. He has been involved at all stages of the project life-cycle from survey to production of concept and detailed designs. Robert has also worked on the client's side in his role with Network Rail in the United Kingdom, as Overhead Line Design Engineer, and during his many secondments into the Public Transport Authority in Perth.

Qualification: Bachelor of Engineering (Hons) Aerospace Engineering.

Currently undertaking Coding Bootcamp through UWA in partnership with Trilogy Education Services.
